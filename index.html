    About the Founder

    Mx. Anuj Pandey (They/Them)
    Founder & Director, Kanpur Queer Welfare Foundation

    Mx. Anuj Pandey is a non-binary queer rights activist and grassroots leader who founded KQWF to uplift and support LGBTQIA+ individuals across Kanpur and surrounding districts. Their story reflects resilience, love, and the belief that inclusion begins with visibility.

    With a focus on mental health, HIV awareness, gender identity, and public advocacy, Anuj creates spaces where queerness is celebrated—not hidden.

    “We’re not just creating change—we’re building a future where every queer person feels safe, proud, and powerful.”
